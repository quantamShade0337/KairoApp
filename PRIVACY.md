# Kyro Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2025_

## 1. Data We Collect

- Account info (username, display name)
- Developer profile (bio, GitHub connection)
- Product metadata
- Activity data (page visits, logins)

Kyro does **not collect payment information**.

## 2. Use of Data

Data is used for platform functionality, authentication, product discovery, analytics, and abuse prevention.

## 3. Data Sharing

Kyro does not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers for hosting/analytics, or when required by law.

## 4. Data Retention

Data is retained while the account is active. Deleting your account removes all associated data.

## 5. User Rights

You may access, edit, or delete your account at any time via Settings.

See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
